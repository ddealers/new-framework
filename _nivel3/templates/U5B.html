1. Decide which sentences in each pair is correct, according to the text "The House from Hell" (unit 2).

1 Participants are required to stay in five nights a week. Participants are allowed to stay in five nights a week.
2 Their loved ones are allowed to visit them. Their loved ones have to visit them.
3 Their loved ones are allowed to stay the night. Their loved ones have to stay the night.
4 The participants are required to bring five items with them. The participants are permitted to bring five items with them.
5 They are not allowed to spend more than $400 a week. They don't have to spend more than $400 a week.

7 They have to complete certain jobs. They are allowed to complete certain jobs.
8 If they don't complete the jobs, they can pay a fine. If they don't complete the jobs, they have to pay a fine.
