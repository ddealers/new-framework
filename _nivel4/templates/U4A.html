Are you a technophobe?

1. Are you a technophobe? Do you like new technology or do you prefer to do things the way your parents did them? Choose the best answer to the questions below and find out.

  1. You're writing a letter on your computer. Suddenly the letter disappears from the computer screen.
    Do you...
    a try to discover what the problem is?
    b telephone the shop where you bought the computer?
    c go and finish writing the letter on your typewriter?

  2. Your best friend is telling you about his incredible new DVD player.
    Do you...
    a ask him for the name of the store so you can also buy one?
    b tell him that you prefer the CD player you bought ten years ago?
    c ask what a DVD player is?

  3. In class one day, you hear someone's cell phone ringing.
    Do you...
    a decide that you would like to buy one, too?
    b ask the person to turn it off, because you can't hear the teacher?
    c ask the person where he puts in the money?

  4. It's Christmas. Which of these presents would you prefer?
    a The new scanner that you read about in your computer magazine.
    b A video cassette to record your favorite Christmas television shows.
    c A book—the Christmas holidays are the perfect time to do some reading.

  5. A friend explains that she does a lot of shopping on the Internet.
    You reply...
    a Can I come to your house and do my shopping also?
    b Great, but I prefer going to the mall and talking to people.
    c What's the Internet?

  6. Someone gives you an electric toothbrush for your birthday.
    How do you react?
    a You're very pleased—you've recently read that electric toothbrushes are faster and 20% more efficient than ordinary toothbrushes.
    b You decide that it might be useful when you go on vacation.
    c You give it to your grandad for Christmas.
