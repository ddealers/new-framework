Anyway, I got to Venice, and discovered that the ferry had been cancelled . I had no money , but there was another ferry leaving in a couple of days, so I had to live on bread and cheese for two days. The ferry eventually left, but it was going very slowly and we discovered that it only had one engine ... or the engine wasn't working properly, and going very very slowly, and we took much longer than expected, and this wasn't helped because we had to make a big detour around Crete because there was some military operation happening there. It took a week to get from Venice to Alexandria, by which time I had no food , no money and had slept very badly. I've never been so pleased to stand on dry land again.
