Computer words

1. Do you consider yourself "computer literate"? For example, do you know the meaning of the following terms and abbreviations?

1 browseran Internet application that lets users travel (surf) the World Wide
Web.
2 servera computer that provides a specific kind of service to client computers.
3 cursorthe moving arrow or dash on a computer screen that shows you where
you are.
4 scannera machine for converting pictures, photographs, texts into digital
images.
5 screensavera moving image which is displayed on your computer screen when it is
not being used.
6 Javaa programming language invented by Sun Microsystems that is
specifically designed for writing programs that can be safely
downloaded to your computer through the Internet.
7 WWWWorld Wide Web is a global system of hypertext documents linked
together by the Internet.
8 CD-ROMstands for Compact Disc - Read Only Memory, which means that you
can't change the data on the disk.
9 HTMLHyperText Markup Language - The language used to create Hypertext
documents for use on the World Wide Web.
10 cyberspacethat space where people communicate through electronic computer
networks and where information resources are available.

2. Are you addicted to the Net?

1 Do you stay online longer than you originally planned?
2 Would you rather stay online than go out with friends?
3 Has time spent on the Internet made you neglect household chores?
4 Do you get angry when someone interrupts you when you are online?
5 Do your friends or family accuse you of spending too long on the Internet?
6 Do you feel a need to check your e-mail constantly?
7 Has your work (or your study) suffered because of the time you have spent online?
8 Do you skip meals in order to stay online?
9 Do you get increasingly restless, anxious or irritable the more time you are not online?
10 Are your online friendships more satisfying than your real ones?

Now check your score.

Score 0-15At the moment you are in no risk of Net addiction, but if you are new
to the Internet you may need to check your score in six months' time.
Score 16-25The Net is starting to affect your life in ways that you might not realize
and you are in danger of becoming hooked. It is not too late to take
steps to control your Net use.
Score 20-40You are experiencing frequent problems because of your addiction to
the Net. You need to consider the impact it is having on your life and
the life of your loved ones.
