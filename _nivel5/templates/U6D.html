Will for habits

1. Will for habitual behavior.

He will spend hours at a time on the Internet.
This is another way of saying:
He habitually spends hours at a time on the Internet.

Rewrite these sentences using will.

She habitually watches six hours of TV a day..
On my day off I am in the habit of spending all morning in bed..
On the weekend Tom always gets home at sunrise..
Big brothers habitually tease their younger sisters..
On a good day I usually get to work at nine..
My son is in the habit of playing computer games for hours..
Some birds are in the habit of migrating every winter..
Some dogs bark regularly for no reason..
