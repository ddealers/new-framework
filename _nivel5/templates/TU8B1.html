Word List

alibi (n)
proof that a person was not at the scene of a crime

a cheap hotel for people who usually travel with backpacks - a bag with straps that goes over your shoulders, and is used for carrying clothes and camping equipment

call off (v)
cancel - as in "We decided to call off the party because I was ill"

estranged (adj)
an estranged person no longer lives with his or her partner

impending (adj)
something is impending if it is going to happen soon

insurance assessor (n)
a person whose job it is to calculate damage, etc., to see how much insurance money should be paid

jog one's memory (idm)
something jogs your memory if it reminds you of something you may have forgotten

keyword (n)
a keyword is a word in a text that occurs with a higher frequency than normal, and is therefore an indication as to what the text is about

mount (v)
to grow, increase

odds (n)
the probability of something happening, often expressed as a ratio, e.g. 1:300 or 300-1

overdue (adj)
something is overdue if it is later than expected

secluded (adj)
quiet and private

shoreline (n)
where the sea or a lake meets the land

sighting (n)
if there is a sighting of something or someone it means that something unusual or someone missing has been seen

a mark or sign that shows that something has happened or someone has passed. So trace (v) means to look for traces.

well-being (n)

withdraw (v)
