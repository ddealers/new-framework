2. Read the descriptions of four ball games and click on the name of the sport.

  1. This game is played on a field with a goal at each end. There are two teams, and each team has 11 players. To score a goal you have to kick the ball into the other team's goal. The team with the most goals at the end wins the game.

  2. This game is played on a court with a net in the middle. Two players hit a ball over the net with a racket. The ball can only bounce once in each half of the court. If one player can't return the ball, the other player wins a point.

  3. This game is played on a field with 4 bases. The batter stands on the home base and the pitcher throws a ball at him. The batter has to hit the ball and run around the bases. If he gets back to home base he scores a run. A game has 9 parts called innings.

  4. This game is played on a course with 18 holes. Players hit a ball with a club and have to put the ball into a hole. Every time you hit your ball it is one stroke. The winner is the one with the least strokes.
